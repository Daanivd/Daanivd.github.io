WHO AM I

Raised in The Netherlands, Trained up as a behavioural biologist, passionate about wildlife and photography. I try to combine these passions and my skills to create stunning imagery of the world around me.

IMAGERY

Through my photography, I try to show animals in their environment, exhibiting natural and interesting behaviour. Through technologies such as camera traps, floating hides and underwater housings I try to get me or my camera as close as possible to interesting wildlife.
