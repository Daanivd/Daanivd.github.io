Monotreme sightings accross Australia

Monotremes are a group of mammals that form the order Monotremata. Monotremes are especially odd in that they are the only mammals that lay eggs, but also feed their offspring with milk. Two of the five species of Monotremes are endemic to Australia. These are the Short-beaked Echidna and the Duck-billed Platypus. The Atlas of Living Australia, or ALA, is a repository of information on Australian plants, animals and fungi.
